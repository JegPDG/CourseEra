Aespa my girls and ai

Aespa is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment. The group debuted in November 2020 with their single "Black Mamba." Distinctively, aespa is known for its unique concept, which integrates virtual counterparts or avatars for each of its members, combining the worlds of reality and virtual reality.

About my girls

Winter (real name: Kim Min-jeong) is a South Korean singer and one of the members of the girl group aespa, which was formed by SM Entertainment. She debuted with aespa in November 2020 with their single "Black Mamba."
